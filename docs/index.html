Why Choose Us?

A NETWORK FOR GAMERS

All human beings should be capable of doing what their like for a living. Today, it is inadmissible that gamers cannot do business with the product of hours invested in games.
It is time for gamers to regain control regarding this aspect generating an ecosystem from which developers, designers and gamers benefit equally.

TRANSPARENCY

In the world of cryptocurrencies, many teams have hidden their faces under the banner of privacy cheating thousands of investors who believed in their promises.
Yamana has transparency as a priority. Investors will know who the members of the team are, there will also be bi-weekly videos talking about improvements and the decisions involving the course of the project.

GAMES

Yamana aims at generating solutions for the gamer universe. Thus, we are developing videogames that prove the positive qualities of this network and this system.
In this sense, Yamana Network also intends to consolidate itself as a competent video gaming company for the different mobile, web and console platforms.
